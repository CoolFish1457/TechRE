Our Mission

To engineer an affordable, reliable robotic guide dog that restores independence and mobility for the visually impaired community, powered entirely by a smartphone app for intuitive, modern control.

Vision Beyond Sight

Real-time obstacle detection, curb recognition, and semantic mapping — all streamed to your phone via voice feedback.

App-Powered Control

Voice commands, destination input, and real-time audio guidance through your smartphone — no physical feedback needed.

Autonomous Navigation

LiDAR, depth cameras, and SLAM for indoor/outdoor wayfinding — simply set your destination in the app.

Cutting-Edge Technology

Powered by two ultra soic sensors and seamless smartphone integration

Sensor Fusion

3D LiDAR + stereo vision + IMU. Detects obstacles up to 20m, classifies stairs, crosswalks, and dynamic objects.

Edge AI

NVIDIA Jetson Orin runs YOLOv8 real-time detection & path planning. Voice recognition for destination commands via app.

App Connectivity

Low-latency BLE 5.0 + 5G connectivity. The app sends destinations and receives real-time voice guidance updates.

The GuideDog Companion App

Your complete control center — set destinations, receive voice guidance, and monitor your journey. No leash feedback needed. Just voice and sound.

Audio Guidance Only

Fully Accessible UI

Designed with VoiceOver/TalkBack compatibility, high contrast, and large touch targets for effortless use by visually impaired users.
