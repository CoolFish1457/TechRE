The Meaning Behind Jaga

Jaga (ජාගරණය) — derived from the Sinhala word "Jāgarana" — means alertness, vigilance, and wakefulness. It embodies the essence of a guardian who never sleeps, constantly watchful and ready to respond. Just like a protector always keeping watch over those in their care, Jaga serves as an ever-vigilant companion for the visually impaired.

"Always awake, always aware — the eternal guardian."

Our Mission

To create Jaga — an affordable, culturally-rooted robotic guide dog that restores independence and mobility for the visually impaired community, powered entirely by a smartphone app for intuitive, modern control.

Constant Vigilance

Like its name Jaga (ජාගරණය), our robot maintains 24/7 awareness, detecting obstacles and hazards in real-time.

App-Powered Control

Voice commands, destination input, and real-time audio guidance through your smartphone — no physical feedback needed.

Protective Guardian

Jaga embodies the spirit of a watchful protector — always alert, ready to respond, and dedicated to the safety of its user.

Cutting-Edge Technology

Powered by advanced sensors and seamless smartphone integration

Ultra Sonic Sensors

Dual ultra-sonic sensors provide 360° obstacle detection up to 15m, ensuring Jaga never misses a potential hazard.

Edge AI

Real-time path planning and voice recognition for destination commands via app. Jaga thinks and responds instantly.

App Connectivity

Low-latency BLE 5.0 + 5G connectivity. The app sends destinations and receives real-time voice guidance updates.

The Jaga Companion App

Your complete control center — set destinations, receive voice guidance, and monitor your journey. No leash feedback needed. Just voice and sound.
